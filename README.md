## Investigating Latent Representations in Pre-trained Visual Models ##

Deep Learning computer vision models have achieved high performance on image classification tasks, leveraged by architectural advancements, sophisticated training methods, and the use of large datasets \cite{voulodimos2018deep}. However, traditional evaluation metrics, such as accuracy, provide limited insight into the behavior of these models and cannot characterize their internal representations, organization, or the factors that lead to divergences across distinct scenarios \cite{raghu2021vision}. Thus, the analysis of these internal representations is essential to comprehend aspects such as robustness, generalization, and model interpretability.
